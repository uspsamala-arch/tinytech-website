About us

Academics Meet Industry.

TinyTech IT Solutions is a corporate IT training and technology leadership development organization founded by seasoned IT managers, technical leads, and industry professionals with real experience delivering enterprise-scale solutions across global organizations.

With combined expertise spanning multiple technologies, project management methodologies, and leadership roles, we equip students, fresh graduates, and working professionals with the practical skills today's technology industry actually demands.
